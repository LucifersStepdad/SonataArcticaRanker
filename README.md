Y'arright lads? You like Sonata Arctica, don't you? You wanna spend a full afternoon ranking literally every single Sonata Arctica song? Well do I have the repository for you!

In this simple (vibe coded) little applet thing, you can rank every Sonata Arctica song ever definitively in the tournament format!

There are two ways to do it, the full path, which ranks every single song perfectly - or the ELO path, which is a lot quicker.

The ELO path is much quicker and gives a really good approximation of your perfect final list but it isn't as powerful as the full path.

Then of course you get all your results, which album order you actually like best vs which you said you liked best (IS Unia the greatest album ever written...?)

It'll give you a fun little tier list, which is nice and of course it'll give you a full list so you can compare with all your friends their results!

So sit back, get some vodka, and spend the afternoon thinking about Sonata Arctica. It's better than working!
